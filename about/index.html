Hi there! I am a graduate student at Imperial College London pursuing an MSc in Computing with specialism in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Recently, I joined the Brain and Behaviour Lab working under the supervision of Professor Aldo Faisal on Reinforcement Learning control.

I have a diverse set of interests. Currently, I am particularly interested in how we can leverage our understanding of human locomotion to develop better control algorithms. In parallel to my MSc Thesis, I am also working with Dr Julia Ive in the NLP group on multi-modal neural language models and transfer learning.

After graduating from ETH Zurich with a BSc in Civil Engineering, I worked at Amazon as a Research Scientist intern on large scale supervised learning algorithms and capacity optimisation. I also had the chance to work as a Venture Capital intern at Global Founders Capital where I sourced deals around the world and met some awesome Startup founders! My first internship after graduating was at construyo, where I helped Fabian and Leonhard towards their mission to digitize the construction industry.
